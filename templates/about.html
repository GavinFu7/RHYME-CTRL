ABOUT RAP RHYME HIGHLIGHTER

A local tool for visualizing rap rhyme schemes, timing, and wordplay.

What it does

This app helps you see how a verse is built:

  • Text mode colorizes rhyme families directly from pasted lyrics.
  • Auto-align mode runs Whisper on your audio, aligns your lyrics to the performance, and scrolls the bar-by-bar view in sync.
  • Track storage saves each run in a local SQLite database so you can inspect or analyze it later.

How rhyme detection works

Rhyme detection is based on phonemes, not just spelling. The app uses the CMU Pronouncing Dictionary and a fuzzy similarity score that:

  • Looks at the last few phonemes of each word.
  • Gives extra weight to the last stressed vowel (to capture assonance).
  • Groups words into rhyme families when the score exceeds a configurable threshold.

That means near-rhymes and bent pronunciations—like egotistic / gifted—can land in the same family when you use a looser threshold.

How audio alignment works

In auto-align mode, the pipeline is roughly:

  • Run Whisper on the audio to get a sequence of time-coded segments.
  • Align your pasted lyric lines to those segments using greedy text similarity.
  • Attach rhyme labeling and drive a scrolling, synchronized lyric view while the audio plays.

Data & privacy

Everything is designed to run locally:

  • Audio is processed on your machine via Whisper.
  • Tracks are stored in a local SQLite file at data/rhyme_highlighter.db.
  • Uploaded audio lives under static/uploads/ next to the app.
